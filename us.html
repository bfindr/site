Our story

From Montreal5A7 to bfindr

In 2014, seeing the need for an information service in the domain of business events,

we created Montreal 5a7, today the premier specialized event agenda in greater Montreal.


To maximize on this service, we are launching bfindr in Spring 2017, a smart platform matching people to events.
bfindr finds participants and companies the ideal events and helps organizers unite the right people.
